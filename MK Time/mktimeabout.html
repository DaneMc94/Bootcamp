Our Brand

MK Time is a Swiss watch company with a rich heritage dating back to 1920. Founded by master watchmaker Martin Koenig in the picturesque Swiss town of Lucerne, the company quickly gained recognition for its meticulous craftsmanship and attention to detail. In 1950, the company expanded its operations to Edinburgh, blending Swiss precision with Scottish engineering, a marriage that remains at the heart of our brand today.

Our unique selling proposition is offering luxury and refinement at affordable prices. We are committed to creating timepieces that not only tell time but also tell a story. Each MK Time watch is a testament to our dedication to elegance, sophistication, and quality. With our commitment to Swiss precision and Scottish craftsmanship, we aim to deliver watches that not only meet but exceed the expectations of our discerning customers.

To experience the timeless elegance and class of MK Time, explore our collection today.
